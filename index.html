About Us

Our Mission

mini marvels is the first Center in Mysore to provide holistic services in the field of pediatric intervention. Our aim is to ensure that detection and intervention of communication and swallowing disorders are done as early as possible so that every child with developmental challenges has an opportunity to become independent and live life to the best of their abilities.

About the Founder

Ms. Archana Marigowda has completed BASLP (Bachelors in Audiology and Speech-Language Pathology) from the All India Institute of Speech and Hearing, Mysore, and MScSLP (Masters in Speech-language Pathology) from Dr. S.R. Chandrasekhar Institute of Speech and Hearing, Bangalore. She has spent the last three and a half years working in the field of pediatric rehabilitation and is an experienced practitioner of play-based therapy strategies. She has in-depth knowledge and expertise in the assessment and treatment of Autism Spectrum Disorders.

She has been trained by experts in assessing and treating various Oro-motor, Feeding, and motor planning challenges such as Apraxia of Speech, using Oral Placement Therapy, and PROMPT, play-based approaches such as the Hanen Program, DIRFloortime, and the SonRise Program, and sensory-based approaches like Sensory Integration.

Ms.Archana is one of the founding members of Proerehab, an online pediatric intervention team, which has been delivering tele-consultation and rehabilitation services in the field of Speech-language therapy, Occupational therapy and Physiotherapy since April, 2020.

Services

The following therapies and intervention strategies are available for children with special needs:

* Speech and language therapy

* Feeding and swallowing therapy

* ‘Play therapy’

* ‘Infant stimulation’

* Telerehabilitation (online therapies for clients staying outside Mysore)

* In-home therapy (exclusive to Mysore)
